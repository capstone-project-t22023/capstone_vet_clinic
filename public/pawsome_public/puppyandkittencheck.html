Puppy & Kitten Check

Puppy & Kitten Check

Welcome to PawsomeVet's Puppy and Kitten Check-Up Section! We understand that your newest furry family members require special care and attention as they embark on their exciting journey of growth and development. Our dedicated team of veterinarians is here to provide comprehensive check-ups tailored to the unique needs of your puppies and kittens.


Puppy Check-Up

  • 1. Physical Examination

During your puppy's initial visit, our veterinarians will conduct a thorough physical examination to assess your puppy's overall health. We'll check for any signs of abnormalities, growth issues, or potential health concerns.

  • 2. Vaccination Schedule

We'll create a personalized vaccination schedule for your puppy based on their age, breed, and potential exposure risks. Vaccinations are essential to protect your puppy from various contagious diseases.

  • 3. Parasite Prevention

Parasites like fleas, ticks, and intestinal worms can affect your puppy's health. Our team will recommend and provide appropriate preventive measures to keep your pup safe.

  • 4. Diet and Nutrition

Proper nutrition is crucial for your puppy's growth and development. We'll discuss dietary recommendations and provide guidance on feeding schedules, portion sizes, and nutritional requirements.

  • 5. Behavioral Guidance

Puppyhood is a critical time for socialization and training. Our experts can offer advice on behavior training, socialization with other animals and people, and tips to ensure your puppy grows up to be a well-adjusted and happy companion.

Kitten Check-Up


  • 1. Comprehensive Health Assessment

Our veterinarians will conduct a thorough health assessment of your kitten, checking for any physical abnormalities, growth patterns, and signs of potential health issues.

  • 2. Vaccination Plan

Based on your kitten's age, breed, and lifestyle, we'll create a customized vaccination plan to safeguard them against common feline diseases.

  • 3. Parasite Control

Kittens are susceptible to parasites such as fleas, ticks, and intestinal worms. We'll provide effective preventive solutions to protect your kitten and your household.

  • 4. Nutritional Consultation

A balanced diet is vital for your kitten's proper growth and development. We'll provide recommendations on the best kitten food, feeding schedules, and dietary choices.

  • Litter Box and Behavior Advice

e understand the importance of proper litter box training and behavior development in kittens. Our team can offer guidance on litter training, scratching behavior, and other essential behavioral aspects.


At PawsomeVet, we are dedicated to ensuring your puppies and kittens receive the best care possible during their crucial early stages. Our goal is to support you in raising happy, healthy, and well-adjusted pets. If you have any questions or concerns about your puppy's or kitten's health, behavior, or well-being, our experienced veterinarians are here to provide the guidance you need. Remember, early veterinary care sets the foundation for a lifetime of well-being for your furry companions. Schedule your puppy or kitten's check-up with PawsomeVet today!
