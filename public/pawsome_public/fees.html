Fees & Insurance

Fees & Insurance


At PawsomeVet, we believe in providing high-quality veterinary care that is both effective and affordable. We understand that your pet's health is important to you, and we are committed to offering transparent fee structures and guidance on insurance options to ensure your peace of mind.





Consultation Fees

  1. Standard Consultation: Our standard consultation fee covers general check-ups, routine vaccinations, and minor health concerns. This fee starts at approximately $60 AUD and may vary depending on the type of pet and the nature of the visit.
  2. Specialist Consultation: If your pet requires specialized care, we offer consultations with our experienced specialists. These consultations may involve advanced diagnostics and treatments, and the fee will reflect the level of expertise required. Prices for specialist consultations start at around $120 AUD.

Diagnostic Services

  1. Laboratory Tests: Prices for laboratory tests, such as blood work, urine analysis, and pathology, can vary based on the specific tests needed for your pet's condition. Costs for basic tests start at approximately $50 AUD.
  2. Imaging (X-rays, Ultrasounds): Diagnostic imaging services are available to identify underlying health issues. Costs for imaging services start at around $100 AUD, depending on the type of imaging and the area being examined.

Procedures and Treatments

  1. Surgical Procedures: The cost of surgical procedures, including spaying, neutering, and more complex surgeries, will depend on the type of procedure and the size of your pet. Prices for surgical procedures start at approximately $150 AUD.
  2. Dental Care: Dental procedures, cleanings, and extractions are essential for your pet's oral health. Fees for these services are determined based on the extent of dental work required and start at around $120 AUD.

Medications and Preventatives

  1. Prescription Medications: If your pet requires medication, we provide prescribed medications to help treat various conditions. Prices vary based on the medication type and dosage, with costs typically ranging from $20 to $100 AUD.
  2. Preventative Products: We offer a range of flea, tick, and heartworm preventatives to ensure your pet's ongoing health. Prices for these products will vary depending on the type and size of your pet, starting at approximately $15 AUD.

Insurance

We strongly recommend considering pet insurance to provide financial security for unexpected veterinary expenses. While PawsomeVet doesn't endorse specific insurance providers, we encourage you to explore various insurance options to find coverage that suits your pet's needs and your budget.


Payment Options

  1. Payment Plans: In some cases, we offer payment plans to help manage the cost of larger treatments or procedures. Please inquire with our staff to learn about available options.
  2. Accepted Payment Methods: We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and online payments.

Please note that the above prices are approximate and provided in AUD. For accurate and up-to-date fee details, as well as information about insurance options, please contact our clinic directly. We are here to provide the best care for your furry companions while ensuring transparency and affordability in our services.
