Health Concerns Consultations

Dermatology Pathology

In both dogs and cats, skin conditions are quite prevalent. You don't have to put up with your dog or cat constantly ripping out hair or scratching or licking it. We can assist!

Weight Loss Management

Our Weight management Clinic is a no-cost service that aims to give you access to a systematic weight management program that is tailored just for your pet. Our expert veterinary nurses will create a diet plan to assist your pet in losing weight and establish a realistic target for them. Then, your pet's progress will be monitored with twice-weekly weigh-ins, and their weight loss plan will be adjusted as needed. Call the clinic and ask to talk with Jana or Rosy for further details.

Behaviour Councelling

Numerous pets have behavioural disorders that seriously impact both them and their owners for a variety of reasons. A veterinary behaviourist who is qualified to address any worries you or your pet may have can be referred to by one of our hospitals' veterinarians if we are unable to resolve your issue.

Chiropractor Services

Our team of skilled and certified animal chiropractors brings specialized expertise in evaluating and addressing musculoskeletal conditions in pets. With a deep understanding of animal anatomy and biomechanics, our chiropractors are dedicated to providing safe and effective chiropractic care.
