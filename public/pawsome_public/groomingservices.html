Grooming Services

At PawsomeVet, we understand that a well-groomed pet is a happy and healthy pet. Our professional grooming services are designed to enhance your furry friend's appearance, promote hygiene, and contribute to their overall well-being. With a team of experienced groomers and a pet-friendly environment, we offer a range of grooming options to cater to your pet's specific needs.


Grooming Services

  1. Bathing & Shampooing We provide gentle and thorough bathing services using premium pet-friendly shampoos and conditioners. Our experienced groomers ensure that your pet's coat is cleaned, moisturized, and free from dirt and odors.
  2. Coat Trimming and Styling Whether your pet needs a full haircut, a trim, or just some tidying up, our groomers are skilled in various coat styles and techniques. We consult with you to understand your preferences and your pet's needs, ensuring a style that both you and your pet will love.
  3. Nail Trimming Regular nail trims are essential for your pet's comfort and mobility. Our groomers carefully trim your pet's nails to an appropriate length, avoiding discomfort or injury.
  4. Ear Cleaning Ear hygiene is crucial to prevent infections and discomfort. Our gentle ear cleaning services help maintain your pet's ear health and reduce the risk of ear-related issues.
  5. Teeth Brushing Dental care is vital for your pet's overall health. We offer teeth brushing services to help keep your pet's teeth clean, reducing the risk of dental diseases and promoting fresh breath.
  6. De-shedding Treatments If your pet sheds excessively, our de-shedding treatments can help reduce shedding and keep your home cleaner. Our specialized techniques help manage shedding without harming your pet's coat.
  7. Anal Gland Expression Anal gland issues can be uncomfortable for your pet. Our groomers can perform anal gland expression, providing relief and reducing the risk of related problems.
  8. Spa Packages Treat your pet to the ultimate pampering experience with our spa packages. These packages combine various grooming services, ensuring your pet looks, feels, and smells their best.
  9. Specialized Care If your pet has specific grooming needs due to their breed, coat type, or medical conditions, our groomers are trained to provide specialized care tailored to your pet's unique requirements.
  10. Stress-Free Environment We prioritize your pet's comfort and well-being during the grooming process. Our facility is designed to create a calm and stress-free environment, minimizing anxiety and ensuring a positive experience.

At PawsomeVet, our grooming services go beyond just aesthetics. We believe that regular grooming contributes to your pet's physical health and emotional well-being. Our skilled groomers are passionate about providing personalized care that leaves your pet looking and feeling their best. Contact us today to schedule a grooming appointment and give your furry friend the PawsomeVet grooming experience!
