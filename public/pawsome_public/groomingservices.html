Grooming Services

Bathing & Shampooing

We provide gentle and thorough bathing services using premium pet-friendly shampoos and conditioners. Our experienced groomers ensure that your pet's coat is cleaned, moisturized, and free from dirt and odors.

Coat Trimming & Styling

Whether your pet needs a full haircut, a trim, or just some tidying up, our groomers are skilled in various coat styles and techniques. We consult with you to understand your preferences and your pet's needs, ensuring a style that both you and your pet will love.

Nail Trimming

Regular nail trims are essential for your pet's comfort and mobility. Our groomers carefully trim your pet's nails to an appropriate length, avoiding discomfort or injury.

Ear Cleaning

Ear hygiene is crucial to prevent infections and discomfort. Our gentle ear cleaning services help maintain your pet's ear health and reduce the risk of ear-related issues.

Teeth Brushing

Dental care is vital for your pet's overall health. We offer teeth brushing services to help keep your pet's teeth clean, reducing the risk of dental diseases and promoting fresh breath.

De-shedding Treatments

If your pet sheds excessively, our de-shedding treatments can help reduce shedding and keep your home cleaner. Our specialized techniques help manage shedding without harming your pet's coat./p>

Anal Gland Expression

Anal gland issues can be uncomfortable for your pet. Our groomers can perform anal gland expression, providing relief and reducing the risk of related problems.

Spa Packages

Treat your pet to the ultimate pampering experience with our spa packages. These packages combine various grooming services, ensuring your pet looks, feels, and smells their best.
