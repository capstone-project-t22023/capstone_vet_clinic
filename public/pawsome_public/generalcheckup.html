General Consultation

During a general consultation at PawsomeVet, our experienced veterinarians will take the time to thoroughly assess your pet's overall health. We believe in a holistic approach, considering not only the immediate concerns but also your pet's medical history, lifestyle, and any potential environmental factors.

Vaccinations

The majority of dogs in Sydney receive annual vaccinations for kennel cough and a parvovirus vaccine every one to three years. Cats typically receive annual vaccinations against cat flu and parvovirus, while indoor-only cats may only require this vaccination every three years in specific circumstances.

A thorough physical examination and health checkup, including dental and heart exams, are always included with vaccinations. Although annual vaccination is still advised, there are various solutions available based on your pet's habitat or immunity. Send us a note if you'd like to know if your pet needs a vaccination.

Microchipping & Scanning

This is a helpful tool for finding lost pets. It is legally required to get your dog or cat microchipped by the age of 12 weeks or at the time of change of ownership. It is quick, safe, and permanent.
