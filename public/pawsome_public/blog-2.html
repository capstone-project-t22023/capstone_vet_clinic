Why is Digestive Health So Important for Dogs and Cats?

Why is Digestive Health So Important for Dogs and Cats?

  • Mia Thompson
  • Jun 26, 2023

Have you been wondering why the word ‘microbiome’ has become an increasingly popular topic? It is because we now know that the microbes living on and in the body are not just a random cluster of germs that can make an individual sick. The gut microbiome is a miniature ecosystem consisting of billions of living microorganisms that occupy the digestive tract. These play an important role in balancing not only digestive health, but overall health and wellbeing.

Why is a healthy microbiome so important for dogs and cats?

A healthy microbiome is vital for digestive health and helps to keep our pets regular as well as promoting healthy stools. A healthy gut microbiome also has other important benefits including supporting a pet’s immune system, and even helping to prevent disease. The microbes can also unlock and synthesise nutrients that benefit the pet directly. In fact, the cells lining the large intestine get most of their energy from nutrients synthesised this way.

How can we support and promote healthy gut flora?

There are different approaches to influence digestive health, including probiotics (live microbes) and prebiotics (food for bacteria).

Let’s start with probiotic supplementation, an approach you are likely more familiar with. While this is a more common approach, it has some downfalls. Results can vary from pet to pet, mostly as a result of adding foreign bacteria into the gut. These supplements, or food additives, may not be derived from bacteria naturally found in a cat or dog's microbiome.

An alternative approach is to feed prebiotic fibres. These fibres feed and nourish the microbes within the individual pet’s own microbiome to support digestive wellness. Hill’s ActivBiome+ ingredient technology is a proprietary blend of prebiotics including ground pecan shells, citrus pulp, flaxseed, beet pulp and pressed cranberries. Because dogs and cats have to eat every day, feeding a complete and balanced food specifically designed to nourish and support their unique gut microbiome is a practical and long-lasting strategy to support digestive health.
